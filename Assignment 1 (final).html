About

Overview and Program Objective

The Student Development Section focuses on supporting students in both academic and personal growth. This website guides students in leadership development, career readiness, and academic resource accessibility. The program empowers students through mentorship, workshops, and digital resources to enhance their educational journey.

Faculty Profile

The Student Development Section is managed by a team of dedicated faculty members specializing in career counseling, leadership training, and student welfare. Our team includes academic advisors, counselors, management staff, and student representatives who work collaboratively to support individual student needs.

CAMPUS IN CHAOS: STUDENTS STAGE PROTEST OVER UNFAIR RULE—ADMIN STAYS SILENT!

    Students are furious after a sudden rule change that no one saw coming. Overnight, a new policy was enforced—stricter dress codes, earlier curfews, and bizarre fines for minor violations. The worst part? No one was warned.

A group of students took matters into their own hands, storming the admin office with banners and demands. “They can’t just change the rules without telling us,” one student said. “We’re not going to stay quiet.”

Despite the growing tension, the administration has refused to respond. What are they hiding? And will this protest turn into something even bigger?

Stay tuned—this is just getting started.

WHERE DID HE GO? POPULAR STUDENT LEADER DISAPPEARS BEFORE MAJOR EVENT!

    Something weird is going on. A well-known student—the face of multiple clubs, always in the spotlight—has vanished without a trace.

He was last seen at a club meeting three days ago, looking “stressed and distracted.” Since then, his phone has been off, his social media wiped clean, and he even missed a huge event he was supposed to lead. No explanation. No goodbye.

Some say he just needed a break. Others think he’s hiding from something big. But the real question is—did something happen to him?

Whatever the reason, no one is talking… and that’s making people even more suspicious.

EXPOSED: THE SECRET “ELITE CLUB” CONTROLLING CAMPUS OPPORTUNITIES!

    Ever wonder why some students always get the best internships, exclusive event invites, and secret scholarships—while the rest of us struggle just to find out about them? Turns out, there’s a reason.

Rumors are swirling about a private student society that handpicks its members. No applications. No announcements. You’re either in… or you don’t even know it exists.

Even crazier? Someone was allegedly kicked out for leaking details. What else are they hiding? And how deep does this really go?

If you’re not in the circle, you’re already too late.
